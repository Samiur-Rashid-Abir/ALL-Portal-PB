Policies & Fees

Taxes and Duties

  • Tax at Source: 10% for Individuals and 20% for Companies
  • Excise Duty: Annual tiered fees
    • Up to 3 Lac: Nil
    • 3 Lac - 15 Lac: 150 BDT
    • 15 Lac - 1 Cr: 500 BDT
    • 1 Cr - 5 Cr: 2,500 BDT
    • Above 5 Cr: 50,000 BDT
